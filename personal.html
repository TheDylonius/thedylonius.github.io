Video Games

I have always been an avid video gamer, and I'm very competitive when I do play. I enjoy a mix of genres, such as competitive, story-based, MMORPG, and LEGO games!

Counter Strike

My favourite game of all time and probably my largest time sink of all time! I've played in several amateur Counter Strike teams and tournaments winning some small cash prizes, nothing major. I haven't played at such a level in several years now and only occasionally play with friends for fun.

Rocket League

My second favourite game of all time, but I've played it much less than Counter Strike for sure. I'm not even that good at this one despite having a couple thousand hours of playtime, but I do occasionally act like I'm a pro!
